Overall Contest Prizes
For overall performance in all three rounds, the contest’s top eligible† teams will be awarded prizes worth:
1st $750
2nd $500
3rd–5th $100
Per-Round Prizes
For each of the three rounds, the round’s top eligible† teams will be awarded prizes worth:
1st $200
2nd $150
3rd–5th $50

† Teams are eligible (to win prizes) if all of its competitors are middle-schoolers or high-schoolers studying in the United States of America. Ineligible teams will be factored out when awarding prizes.
